## Adding new third-party components

When forking additional files from upstream projects or adding runtime
dependencies:

1. Preserve any existing license headers in forked source files.
2. Add a subsection here identifying the upstream project, its license, and
   the scope of use.
3. Update `scripts/check-headers.sh` to include the newly forked files in
   the header check.
